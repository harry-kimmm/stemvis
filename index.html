flow: export stems from FL (File → Export → split mixer tracks, or solo-bounce each group) → load them into channels → play → DICE + knobs until it feels right → SET IN at the hook → REC. each channel drives its own layer: KICK punches the frame, BASS glows the floor, CLAP flashes, HATS shimmer up top, CHORDS wash the background, ARPS spawn shapes pitched high→top / low→bottom, VOX drives the center ring or your art, FX triggers glitch. keep this tab visible while recording — capture is real-time. LOOK switches the whole visual language. KINETIC hard-cuts between seeded type/image shots on the kicks — CHAOS sets the cut rate (every 8 kicks → every kick), ENERGY adds strobe inverts, WORDS feeds the cuts. ORBIT is the abstract visualizer. POSTER is a still editorial layout. load your own .ttf/.otf font files for either.
